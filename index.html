SaltyTree

Moving softly through any weather 在任何天氣中柔和前行

SaltyTree is a unique kind of tree that knows exactly how to move softly through any weather, welcome the rain to nourish its roots, and grow shining leaves. 鹽樹是一棵很獨特的樹。它深深懂得如何在任何天氣裡溫柔地生活——張開雙臂擁抱雨水來滋養根部，並長出閃閃發亮的葉子。

Storms are unpredictable. Sometimes we don’t know where to start, and we bottle up our troubles and feelings inside. We might feel overwhelmed, shut down, or feel like we have lost our bright, salty-like spark. 生活中的風暴總是叫人措手不及。有時候，我們真的不知道該從何面對，只能把所有的煩惱和情緒都壓抑在心底。這讓我們感到不知所措、封閉了自己，甚至覺得自己失去了原本那份明亮、如鹽般獨特的閃耀光芒。

Art therapy helps clients to externalize their feelings onto an artwork and make space for their natural joy to bloom again. 藝術治療就像是一個溫柔的出口，陪你把心裡那些沉重的感受，一筆一畫地實體化在藝術作品上。當你把情緒釋放出來後，心靈就有空間騰出來，讓內心深處最真實的快樂，重新像花一樣盛開。

"Wearing the storm. Ready for any weather." 「與風浪共舞，無懼晴雨。」
SaltyTree Story 鹽樹的故事

At SaltyTree, we help individuals reconnect with their innate sense of wonder, creativity, and resilience. The name SaltyTree whispers of a tree that moves softly with its landscape, turning a salted ground into its own unique expression. We are all just like those graceful trees. 在「鹽樹」，我們陪伴每個人重新找回與生俱來的內在好奇心、創造力與韌性。「鹽樹」這個名字，低語著一棵樹的故事：它在所處的大地景致中溫柔舒展，將含有鹽分的土壤，轉化為自己獨一無二的生命姿態。我們每一個人，其實都正如那些真實而獨特的樹木。

We are never the burdens we carry. In SaltyTree Art Therapy, you are never viewed as the weight itself. When storms bring worry, anger, or fear, those feelings are held gently as passing weather of the soul. 我們永遠不等於自己所背負的重擔。在鹽樹藝術治療中，我們從不將你簡化為那些壓力與重量。當生命中的風暴帶來焦慮、憤怒或恐懼，那些情緒都將被溫柔地接納，它們不過是，一場終將過去的短暫風雨。

Through different art media and expressions, we help our clients gently channel those stormy currents into their art and give us a safe space to navigate the storm together, so they can untangle the wind from their own branches. 透過各式各樣的藝術媒介與表達方式，我們陪伴你將心頭那些暴烈的風雨暗流，溫柔地導向創作之中。我們一起營造一個安心的空間共同航行、度過風浪，好讓你能從自己的枝幹中解開糾纏的風雨。

Services 服務項目

CREATIVITY HEALS 創造力療癒人心

Art therapy requires no experience. Through the arts, clients can safely express the feelings that words cannot find. We invite different art media, such as paint, clay, collage, and raw materials to help you reshape your inner weather. 藝術治療不需要任何經驗。透過藝術，你能安全地表達言語無法言喻的感受。我們引入不同的藝術媒介，如顏料、陶泥、拼貼及大自然原生材料，陪伴你重塑生命中的天氣。

The Healing Process / Experiencing Art Therapy 治療歷程 / 親歷藝術治療

Our approach gives shape to unexplainable feelings through colors, lines, shapes, dots, or textures, inviting you to express your expressions and/or challenges through different art media when words cannot be found. 我們的陪伴以你為中心。透過色彩、線條、形狀、點點或紋理，我們為難以言喻的感受賦予形體。當言語無法表達時，邀請你藉由不同的藝術媒介，自由表達內心的情感與面臨的挑戰。

Together, we externalize and name the weather. Once your weather is safely sitting on the table as an artwork, you can step back and see that you are the tree, and the storm is just passing through. 我們一同將天氣具體呈現，並為它命名。當你的天氣安全地化為一件藝術品呈現在桌上，你便能退後一步，看見自己才是那棵樹，而風暴僅僅只是路過。

This strengths-based journey allows us to safely explore your creations. Together, we will share new perspectives, get to know a brand-new self, embrace those heavy and lost moments, and jointly discover your unique sunny moments—those times when you feel brave, peaceful, or show a bright smile. 這趟著重你個人長處的歷程，讓我們得以安全地探索你的創作。我們將一起分享新的視角、認識全新的自己，包容那些沉重與迷惘的時刻，並共同發掘專屬的陽光時刻(那些你感到勇敢、平靜、或展露燦爛笑容的瞬間)。

Art Therapist 藝術治療師

Chan, Yan Tsz (Beverly) 陳欣慈 (Beverly)

Beverly is an art therapist and an artist. She is a graduate of the Vancouver Art Therapy Institute. Beverly 是一位藝術治療師和藝術家。她畢業於溫哥華藝術治療學校。

In her practice, she has supported individuals from diverse backgrounds including those with learning differences, health challenges, changes in memory and cognition, and experiences of personal loss. Over time, she has come to see how each person’s story holds the power to heal, to connect, and to influence others. 在她的執業歷程中，她曾支援來自不同背景的人士，包括有學習差異、健康挑戰、記憶與認知變化，以及經歷個人喪失的個案。隨著時間的推移，她看見每個人的故事都擁有治癒、連結及影響他人的力量。

In her sessions, she offers a welcoming space that facilitates artmaking through a person-centred, strengths-based philosophy. She believes art therapy is a gift for everyone —small acts of artmaking open space to communicate, reflect, and foster resilience. 在治療節中，她提供一個溫慢包容的空間，以「以人為本」和「優勢導向」的哲學促進藝術創作。她深信藝術治療是送給每個人的禮物——微小的藝術創作行為能為溝通、反思與培養韌性騰出空間。
