SaltyTree 鹽樹

Finding stability through the weather of life 在任何天氣中尋得安定

SaltyTree is a tree that knows how to stand in any weather. It is a space inspired by the resilience of trees that weather life’s storms, honoring the way they anchor deep into their roots for stability, holding ground, and breathing through each season. 「鹽樹」是一棵懂得在任何天氣中佇立的樹。這是一個受大自然風暴中生存的樹木所啟發的空間，將根深深錨定以求安定、堅守崗位，並在每個季節中深呼吸。

We recognize that the storms we face are often unpredictable and relentless. When you tuck your troubles away or feel numb, we honor these as necessary acts of self-protection, as your mind and body’s deep wisdom takes care of you. 我們所面臨的風暴是不可預測且連綿不斷的。有時候，出於保護自己的本能需求，我們會把煩惱收起來或變得麻木。我們敬重這些渴望保護自己的麻木與沉靜時刻，明白這並非失去力量，而是你的身心正運用深刻的智慧在守護著你。

Here, there is no "right" way to be or to create. We see our role as co-creating this space with you, where your expertise in your own life acts as the compass for our work together. We offer an adaptable environment to welcome both heavy and quiet feelings into creative expression, where your path unfolds entirely at your own pace. 在這裡，沒有所謂的「正確」表達方式。我們視自己為與你「共創空間」的夥伴，你才是自身生命中最深刻的專家，我們將這份專業視為引導這趟旅程的指南針，讓你依循自己的步調，自然綻放。

The SaltyTree Story 鹽樹的故事

At SaltyTree, we explore, discover, and reconnect with your capacity for wonder and resilience. The name SaltyTree whispers of a tree that moves softly with its landscape, turning a harsh, salted ground into its own unique expression. 在「鹽樹」，我們一同探索、發掘並重新連結那份驚奇感、創造力與韌性。「鹽樹」這個名字，細訴著一棵在特定地景中尋得自身出路的樹，它將嚴酷、充滿鹽分的土地，轉化為自己獨一無二的生命展現。

We know no tree grows in a vacuum; our growth is shaped by the complex terrain we inhabit. We acknowledge that the soil you stand in, encompassing the environments, systemic climates, and relationships surrounding you, directly impacts your experience. 然而，我們也深知沒有一棵樹能完全孤立於生態之外；我們的抽枝發芽，往往深深受到所處環境與土壤的形塑。我們深知您站立的土壤，包括您身處的環境、體制氣候以及周圍的人際關係，都直接影響著您的日常體驗。

We are never the burdens we carry. When storms bring worry, anger, or fear, whether born from personal hardships or the heavy, unfair climates of the world around us, those feelings are held gently as the ever-shifting weather of the soul. We work to help you find a place to stand, however small or temporary, within whatever climate you are currently facing. 我們永遠不等於自己所背負的重擔。在藝術治療中，我們從不將自己或彼此視為重量本身。當風暴帶來憂慮、憤怒或恐懼時，無論這份沉重是源於個人的生命課題，抑或是來自外界不公與體制環境的洗禮，那些情緒都僅被溫柔地視為心靈交織的氣候與日常天氣。我們致力於陪伴你在所處的氣候中，找到一個可以站立的位置，無論那空間多麼微小或短暫。

Services 服務項目

Creativity Heals 創造力帶來療癒

Art therapy is a practice of making space, even when your physical environment feels limited, shared, or restrictive. Whether you are meeting us in person or online, no artistic experience is required. 即便你的物理環境受限、共享或不夠寬敞，藝術治療依然能為你開闢一片空間。不論我們是面對面或是線上相會，都不需要任何藝術背景與經驗。

The Healing Process / Experiencing Art Therapy 治療歷程 / 親歷藝術治療

We prioritize your privacy and comfort, working with whatever materials you have on hand, ranging from professional studio supplies to everyday objects or digital tools found in your own space. We honor the dignity of your current circumstances and work to ensure that your creative expression remains a safe, private, and valid experience. 我們提供多元且彈性的表達管道，陪伴你將內在風景具象化，並重新形塑。我們尊重你所處的現實，確保你在任何環境下，都能在隱私與安全感中進行創作，讓你的感受與表達得到充分的尊重與肯定。

Our approach gives physical form to complex feelings through colors, lines, shapes, and sensory exploration. Together, we bring your inner weather and your relationship with the world outside onto the paper, table, or into the physical interaction with materials. Whether an expression is built, layered, dismantled, or simply felt, you can allow the creative process to carry that weight. 我們的諮商方式透過色彩、線條、形狀與質地，將那些難以言喻的複雜感受賦予可見的形狀。我們會一同將你內在的天氣、以及你與外在世界的互動關係，帶到與媒材的互動中。無論這份表達最終是被建立、堆疊、拆解，抑或僅僅是純粹的感官抒發，你都能讓這個創造的歷程去承載那份重量。

This strength-based journey is anchored in honoring your process. We are here to walk alongside you as partners, helping you reconnect with your inherent strengths and uncover whatever you need most in this moment, such as a quiet glimmer of peace, the permission to simply rest, or a newfound sense of vitality, voice, and internal power. 這趟以優勢為導向的旅程，深深錨定於依循您個人步調來敬重並接納您的表達之上。我們是您的夥伴，將與您一同經歷那些沉重與迷惘的時刻，協助您重新連結與生俱來的力量，尋得您在當下季節中最需要的狀態，不論那是重獲寧靜、全然休息的允許，抑或是找回久違的生命活力、內在聲音與內在力量。

Art Therapist 藝術治療師

Chan, Yan Tsz (Beverly) 陳欣慈 (Beverly)

Beverly is an art therapist and an artist. She is a graduate of the Vancouver Art Therapy Institute. Beverly 是一位藝術治療師和藝術家。她畢業於溫哥華藝術治療學校。

In her practice, she has supported individuals from diverse backgrounds including those with learning differences, health challenges, changes in memory and cognition, and experiences of personal loss. Over time, she has come to see how each person’s story holds the power to heal, to connect, and to influence others. 在她的執業歷程中，她曾支援來自不同背景的人士，包括有學習差異、健康挑戰、記憶與認知變化，以及經歷個人喪失的個案。隨著時間的推移，她看見每個人的故事都擁有治癒、連結及影響他人的力量。

In her sessions, she offers a welcoming space that facilitates artmaking through a person-centred, strengths-based philosophy. She believes art therapy is a gift for everyone, knowing that small acts of artmaking open space to communicate, reflect, and foster resilience. 在治療節中，她提供一個溫慢包容的空間，以以人為本和優勢導向的哲學促進藝術創作。她深信藝術治療是送給每個人的禮物，微小的藝術創作行為能為溝通、反思與培養韌性騰出空間。
