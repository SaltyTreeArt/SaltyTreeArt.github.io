Salty Tree Art Therapy

Moving softly through any weather 在任何天氣中柔和前行

Salty Tree is a special kind of tree that knows exactly how to move softly through any weather, welcome the rain to nourish its roots, and grow shining leaves. 鹽樹是一種特別的樹，它深諳如何在任何天氣中柔和前行，擁抱雨水以滋養其根，並長出閃耀的葉子。

Storms are unpredictable. Sometimes we don’t know where to start, and we bottle up our problems and feelings inside. We might feel overwhelmed, shut down, or feel like we have lost our bright, salty-like spark. 風暴是不可預測的。有時我們不知從何著手，將問題與感受封鎖於心裡。我們可能會感到不知所措、封閉自我，或覺得失去了原本明亮且如鹽般的閃耀火花。

Art therapy helps clients to externalize their feelings onto an artwork and make space for their natural joy to bloom again. 藝術治療協助案主將感受具體呈現於藝術作品上，重新為內在的自然喜悅騰出綻放的空間。

Salty Tree Story 鹽樹的故事

At Salty Tree, we help individuals reconnect with their innate sense of wonder, creativity, and resilience. The name Salty Tree whispers of a tree that moves softly with its landscape, turning a salted ground into its own unique expression. We are all just like those graceful trees. 在「鹽樹」，我們協助每個人重新連結與生俱來的奇妙感、創造力與韌性。「鹽樹」之名細訴著一棵與周邊風景柔和共進、隨風而動的樹，將鹽漬的土壤化作自身獨特的生命表達。我們都像這些優雅的樹木一樣。

We are never the burdens we carry. In Salty Tree Art Therapy, you are never viewed as the weight itself. When storms bring worry, anger, or fear, those feelings are held gently as passing weather of the soul. 我們並非我們所面臨的問題本身。在藝術治療中，我們永遠不會被視為問題所在。我們將擔憂、憤怒或恐懼視為外來訪客。

Through different art media and expressions, we help our clients gently channel those stormy currents into their art and give us a safe space to navigate the storm together, so they can untangle the wind from their own branches. 透過不同的藝術媒介與表達，我們引導案主將這些風暴般的亂流溫柔地導向其藝術創作中，提供一個安全的空間共同航行、度過風浪，好讓他們能從自己的枝幹中解開糾纏的風雨。

Services 服務項目

How We Create and Heal 我們如何創作與治癒

Art therapy requires no experience. Through the arts, clients can safely express the feelings that words cannot find. We invite different art media, such as paint, clay, collage, and raw materials to help you reshape your inner weather. 藝術治療不需要任何經驗。透過藝術，案主能安全地表達言語無法言喻的感受。我們引入不同的藝術媒介，如顏料、陶泥、拼貼及大自然原生材料，陪伴您重塑內在的天氣。

The Healing Process / Experiencing Art Therapy 治療歷程 / 親歷藝術治療

Our person-centered approach gives shape to unexplainable feelings through colors, lines, shapes, dots, or textures, inviting you to express your expressions and/or challenges through different art media when words cannot be found. 我們以人為本的治療法，透過色彩、線條、形狀、點點或紋理，為難以言喻的感受賦予形體，邀請您在言語無法表達時，藉由不同的藝術媒介，表達您的情感與/或面對的挑戰。

Together, we externalize and name the weather. Once your weather is safely sitting on the table as an artwork, you can step back and see that you are the graceful tree, and the storm is just passing through. 我們一同將天氣具體呈現並為其命名。一旦您的天氣安全地作為一件藝術品呈現在桌上，您便能退後一步，看見自己才是那棵優雅的樹，而風暴僅僅只是路過。

This strengths-based journey allows us to safely explore your creation to share new perspectives and empowering self-insights, helping you discover your own sunbeam moments when you felt brave, calm, or shared a big smile. 這趟以優勢為本的歷程，讓我們得以安全地探索您的創作，分享新的視角與職能自我洞察，協助您發掘專屬自己的陽光時刻——那些您感到勇敢、平靜或展露燦爛笑容的瞬間。

About Beverly 關於陳欣慈

Chan, Yan Tsz (Beverly) 陳欣慈 (Beverly)

Beverly is an art therapist and an artist. She is a graduate of the Vancouver Art Therapy Institute. Beverly 是一位藝術治療師和藝術家。她畢業於溫哥華藝術治療學校（Vancouver Art Therapy Institute）。

In her practice, she has supported individuals from diverse backgrounds including those with learning differences, health challenges, changes in memory and cognition, and experiences of personal loss. Over time, she has come to see how each person’s story holds the power to heal, to connect, and to influence others. 在她的執業歷程中，她曾支援來自不同背景的人士，包括有學習差異、健康挑戰、記憶與認知變化，以及經歷個人喪失的個案。隨著時間的推移，她看見每個人的故事都擁有治癒、連結及影響他人的力量。

In her sessions, she offers a welcoming space that facilitates artmaking through a person-centred, strengths-based philosophy. She believes art therapy is a gift for everyone —small acts of art-making open space to communicate, reflect, and foster resilience. 在治療節中，她提供一個溫暖包容的空間，以「以人為本」和「優勢導向」的哲學促進藝術創作。她深信藝術治療是送給每個人的禮物——微小的藝術創作行為能為溝通、反思與培養韌性騰出空間。
