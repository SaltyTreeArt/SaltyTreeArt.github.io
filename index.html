Salty Tree Art Therapy 鹽樹藝術治療
Standing tall in any weather 在任何天氣中挺拔屹立

Salty Tree is a special kind of tree that knows exactly how to stand tall, shake off the rain and grow leaves of every color in any kind of weather. 鹽樹是一種特別的樹，它深諳如何挺拔屹立、抖落雨水，並在任何天氣中長出五彩繽紛的葉子。

Storms are unpredictable. Sometimes we don’t know where to start, and we bottle up our problems and feelings inside. We might feel overwhelmed, shut down, or feel like we have lost our bright, salty-like spark. 風暴是不可預測的。有時我們不知道從何開始，把問題和感受封鎖在心裡。我們可能會感到不知所措、封閉自我，或覺得失去了原本明亮且如鹽般的閃耀火花。

Art therapy helps clients to externalize their feelings onto paper and make space for their natural joy to bloom again. 藝術治療幫助案主將感受具象化在紙上，重新為自然的喜悅騰出空間，使其再次綻放。

The Salty Tree Story 鹽樹的故事

Connecting with Wonder & Resilience 重新連結好奇心與韌性

At Salty Tree, we help individuals reconnect with their innate sense of wonder, play, and resilience. The name Salty Tree originates from how young trees learn how to grow strong and vibrant even when the soil around them seems harsh. We are all just like those resilient trees. 在「鹽樹」，我們協助每個人重新連結與生俱來的奇妙感、遊戲心與韌性。這個名字源於小樹如何在看似惡劣的土壤中學會茁壯並充滿活力地成長。我們都像這些充滿生命力的樹木一樣。

"We are never the problems we are facing. In Salty Tree Art Therapy, we are never viewed as the problem. We treat the worry, the anger, or the fear as an external visitor." 「我們並非我們所面臨的問題本身。在Salty Tree藝術治療中，我們永遠不會被視為問題所在。我們將擔憂、憤怒或恐懼視為外來訪客。」

Through different art media and expressions, we help our clients gently channel those big feelings onto the canvas, where we can safely untangle them together. 透過不同的藝術媒介與表達方式，我們引導案主輕柔地將這些巨大的情緒轉移至畫布上，在安全放心的空間裡，一起將它們梳理與解開。

Our Process 我們的歷程

Services: How We Play & Heal 服務：我們如何遊玩與治癒

Art therapy does not require artistic talent. It uses color, textures, and metaphors to safely process what verbal logic cannot reach. 藝術治療不需要任何藝術天賦。它利用色彩、質地與隱喻，安全地處理言語邏輯無法觸及的內在。

DO ARTS AND ENJOY THE PROCESS 進行藝術創作，享受過程

Sometimes feelings have no words and are unexplainable, but we can give them shape through colors, lines, or textures. When we feel angry, worried and upset, we invite our clients to use different art media to express themselves. 有時情緒無法用言語表達、難以解釋，但我們可以透過色彩、線條或紋理為它們塑形。當感到憤怒、憂慮和沮喪時，我們邀請案主使用不同的藝術媒介來表達自己。

We name the storm. Once the storm is sitting on the table, our clients can step back and see that they are the strong tree, and the storm is just passing through. 我們為這場風暴命名。當風暴被具體呈現在桌面上時，案主便能退後一步，看清自己才是那棵強壯的樹，而風暴僅僅只是路過。

Process-Focused 專注過程

EXPRESS, EXPLORE AND REFLECT 表達、探索與反思

We help our clients to look for their sunbeam moments when they felt brave, calm, or shared a big smile. 我們協助案主尋找那些讓他們感到勇敢、平靜或展露燦爛笑容的「陽光時刻」。

We explore and process the artwork together and share new ideas, safe perspectives, and empowering self-insights. 我們一同探索與引導藝術作品的創作歷程，分享新的觀點、安全的視角以及賦予力量的自我洞察。

Strengths-Based 優勢導向

Your Creative Breathing Space 您的創意呼吸空間

I graduated from the Vancouver Art Therapy Institute and hold professional memberships with the Canadian Art Therapy Association (CATA) and the Hong Kong Association of Art Therapists (HKAAT). I also carry deep academic backgrounds in School & Community Psychology and Graphic Design, alongside registered teacher credentials in Hong Kong. 我畢業於加拿大溫哥華藝術治療學校 (VATI)，同時是加拿大藝術治療協會 (CATA) 及 香港藝術治療師協會 (HKAAT) 的專業會員。此外，我也具備學校與社區心理學碩士及平面設計（視覺傳達）學士學位，並持有香港的註冊教師資格（PGDE）。

My clinical practice weaves psychological support, fine arts, and developmental theory. I walk alongside diverse clients, believing fully that individuals are not defined by their challenges. Our objective is to co-create a safe container to untangle worry, anger, or transition fears. 我的臨床執業融合了心理支持、美術與發展理論。我陪伴各種背景的案主，深信「人絕不等於其面臨的挑戰」。我們的目標是共同創造一個安全的容器，以梳理憂慮、憤怒或轉變帶來的恐懼。
