SaltyTree

Finding Stability in Any Weather 在任何天氣中，尋得一份安定

"SaltyTree" is a tree that knows how to stand firmly in any climate. This space is inspired by those trees that strive to survive amidst storms by anchoring their roots deep into the soil and breathing deeply, with resilience, through the changing of each season. 「鹽樹」是一棵懂得在任何氣候中，依然穩穩佇立的樹。這個空間的靈感源自那些在風暴中仍奮力生存的樹木，它們將根深深扎進土壤，在每個季節的變幻中，堅定地呼吸。

The storms we face are often unpredictable and relentless. Sometimes, out of a natural instinct for self-protection, we may choose to close off, or need a bit of space to temporarily distance ourselves from the outside world. We honor these moments of longing for stillness; they are not signs of weakness, but your mind and body utilizing their deepest wisdom to gently protect you. 我們所面對的風暴，往往不可預測且連綿不斷。有時候，出於自我保護的本能，我們會選擇封閉，或是需要一點空間，暫時與外界保持距離。我們敬重這些渴望沉靜的時刻，那並非軟弱，而是你的身心正運用深層的智慧，溫柔地守護著你。

Here, there is no so-called "right" way to express yourself. We are partners co-creating this creative space with you, and you are the most profound expert on your own life. We view your expertise as the compass guiding this journey, allowing you to unfold naturally at your own pace. 在這裡，沒有所謂正確的表達方式。我們是與你共創創意空間的夥伴，而你，才是自身生命中最深刻的專家。我們將這份專業視為引導旅程的指南針，讓你依循自己的步調，自然綻放。

The SaltyTree Story 鹽樹的故事

At SaltyTree, we explore, discover, and reconnect with that sense of wonder, creativity, and resilience together. The name SaltyTree speaks of a tree that finds its way within a specific landscape, transforming a harsh, salt-laden ground into a unique expression of life. 在鹽樹，我們一同探索、發掘並重新連結那份驚奇感、創造力與韌性。鹽樹的名字，訴說著一棵在特定地景中尋得出口的樹，它將嚴酷且充滿鹽分的土地，轉化為獨一無二的生命展現。

Yet, we also deeply know that no tree can grow isolated from the ecosystem; our growth is always shaped by the environment and soil we inhabit. But please remember, you are never the burdens you carry. In art therapy, we do not view ourselves or each other as the weight itself. 然而，我們也深知沒有一棵樹能孤立於生態之外；我們的成長總受到所處環境與土壤的形塑。但請記得，你永遠不等於你所背負的重擔。在藝術治療中，我們不將自己或彼此視為重量本身。

When external challenges bring worry, anger, or fear, whether born from personal hardships or the compression of systemic injustice and the outside world, these emotions are merely clouds and rain passing through the climate of the soul. We are dedicated to walking alongside you, helping you find a place to anchor yourself through creative expression, however small or temporary that space may be. 當外部挑戰帶來憂慮、憤怒或恐懼時，無論這份沉重源於個人的生命課題，或是外界不公與體制的擠壓，這些情緒都只是心靈氣候中飄過的雲雨。我們致力於陪伴你在當下的天氣中，透過創造性表達，找到一個能安身立命的位置，哪怕那個空間再微小、再短暫。

Creativity and Arts 創造力與藝術

Creativity Heals 創造力帶來療癒

Even when environments are restrictive or spaces are shared, art therapy can still carve out a world of your own. We welcome you exactly as you are, no artistic background required. 即便環境受限或空間共享，藝術治療依然能為你開闢一片天地。我們歡迎最真實的你，無需任何藝術背景。

The Healing Process / Experiencing Art Therapy 治療歷程 / 親歷藝術治療

We offer diverse and flexible channels of expression, walking with you as you externalize and reshape your inner landscape. We respect the reality of your circumstances, ensuring that you can create with privacy and a sense of safety under any environment, allowing your every feeling and expression to receive the fullest respect and validation. 我們提供多元且彈性的表達管道，陪伴你將內在風景具象化並重新形塑。我們尊重你所處的現實，確保你在任何環境下，都能在隱私與安全感中創作，讓你的每一份感受與表達，得到最充分的尊重與肯定。

Through color, line, shape, and texture, we give visible form to those complex feelings that are hard to put into words. Together, we bring your inner weather, as well as your connection to the outside world, into a dialogue with the art materials. Whether the final creation is built up, layered, dismantled, or simply serves as a pure sensory release, you can allow the creative process to carry that weight. 透過色彩、線條、形狀與質地，我們將那些難以言喻的複雜感受賦予可見的形狀。我們會一同將你內在的天氣，以及你與外在世界的連結，帶入媒材的對話中。無論創作最終是被構築、堆疊、拆解，或是僅僅是單純的感官抒發，你都能透過這個歷程，讓這份創造去承載那些重量。

This journey centers on your inner strengths, deeply anchored in honoring and accepting your unique pace of expression. We are your partners, willing to support you through those heavy and reflective moments, assisting you to reconnect with your inherent power, and helping you find the state you need most in your current season, whether that is a space to regain tranquility, a permission to fully rest, or a newfound sense of vitality, voice, and internal power. 這趟旅程以你的內在優勢為核心，深深錨定於敬重並接納你個人的表達步調。我們是你的夥伴，願意與你共度那些沉重與沉思的時刻，協助你重新連結與生俱來的力量，找回你在當前季節最需要的狀態，無論是重獲平靜的空間、全然休息的允許，或是重拾活力、發聲與內在力量的契機。

Art Therapist 藝術治療師

Chan, Yan Tsz (Beverly) 陳欣慈 (Beverly)

Beverly is an art therapist and an artist. She is a graduate of the Vancouver Art Therapy Institute. Beverly 是一位藝術治療師和藝術家。她畢業於溫哥華藝術治療學校。

In her practice, she has supported individuals from diverse backgrounds including those with learning differences, health challenges, changes in memory and cognition, and experiences of personal loss. Over time, she has come to see how each person’s story holds the power to heal, to connect, and to influence others. 在她的執業歷程中，她曾支援來自不同背景的人士，包括有學習差異、健康挑戰、記憶與認知變化，以及經歷個人喪失的個案。隨著時間的推移，她看見每個人的故事都擁有治癒、連結及影響他人的力量。

In her sessions, she offers a welcoming space that facilitates artmaking through a person-centred, strengths-based philosophy. She believes art therapy is a gift for everyone, knowing that small acts of artmaking open space to communicate, reflect, and foster resilience. 在治療節中，她提供一個溫慢包容的空間，以以人為本和優勢導向的哲學促進藝術創作。她深信藝術治療是送給每個人的禮物，微小的藝術創作行為能為溝通、反思與培養韌性騰出空間。
